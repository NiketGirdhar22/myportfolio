## Password - CTF x Escape Room

Password, organised by LUG-VITc was a 2 day event comprising of excitting workshops and CTF challanges. The event was attened by almost 300 students from and around Chennai. 

Workshops focused on topics like Linux, Web Exploitation, Binary Explitation, Reverse Engineering and much more exciting topics. Apart from this, there were live demonstrations of Wi-Fi hacking and windows password bypassing. 

The CTF challanges were held on day 2 where participants applied the skills and knowledge they gained in workshops to use and solve the given problems. 

I was in the management team for the event and we were responsible for all the requirement participants might have so as to ensure a smooth flow of the event.
