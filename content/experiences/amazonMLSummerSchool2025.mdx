## Amazon ML Summer School 2025

Amazon ML Summer School 2025 is an integrated learning experience that will helps learn about key machine learning (ML) topics. The curriculum will cover the fundamental concepts while linking them to practical industry applications through an immersive experience. The program is a great opportunity for students to learn from and interact with Scientists at Amazon who have immense knowledge in their ML domain. 

## Qualifier Round

For all registered participants, a test was held which had 2 sections:

- Section 1: Beginner level questions for ML, Probability and basic Aptitute based MCQs
- Section 2: Coding Round, where participants had to solve 2 given questions.
